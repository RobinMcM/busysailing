# How to Get Free Realistic 3D Avatars

## Option 1: Ready Player Me (FREE - Recommended)

1. **Go to:** https://readyplayer.me/avatar
2. **Create two avatars:**
   - Female consultant (professional look, business attire)
   - Female partner (slightly different appearance)
3. **Export settings:**
   - Format: GLB
   - Body type: Half body (upper body only)
   - Quality: High
4. **Download the .glb files**
5. **Place them in:** `client/public/avatars/`
   - Name them: `consultant.glb` and `partner.glb`

## Option 2: Purchase Professional Avatars

If you want higher quality:
- **Sketchfab:** https://sketchfab.com/search?q=female+avatar&type=models
  - Filter: Downloadable, GLB/GLTF format
  - Cost: $20-200 per model
- **TurboSquid:** Similar pricing and selection

## What We Need

The avatars should have:
- ✅ Realistic human face
- ✅ Facial blend shapes/morph targets for mouth movements (jaw open, lips)
- ✅ Upper body (head, neck, shoulders)
- ✅ Professional appearance

## Once You Have the Files

1. Create folder: `client/public/avatars/`
2. Place your .glb files there as:
   - `consultant.glb`
   - `partner.glb`
3. The system will automatically load and animate them

## Cost

- **Ready Player Me:** FREE
- **Purchased avatars:** $50-400 one-time (optional)
- **Running cost:** $0 (loaded once, reused forever)
